Welcome to friendly planner! :)

Plan events with friends, figure out availability, and share the memories!

What is it?

Since it's our senior year, we have to hang out!!! A lot !!! So to practice my website making I made this. It's kinda like a google calender, but we can also put a wishlist of things we wanna do and you can input availability so that we know when to meet. After we meet, you can also upload any pictures you took!!

How do I use it?

  • First, create a friendgroup. This will give a unique key that you can share with as many friends as you want.
  • There's a wishlist. You can add events you want to do, but don't know when to plan it. When you want to plan it, you can click add event.
  • There's a calender. Each member in the group can input availability for each day. This can be changed at any time. When placing an event, it will suggest places where all friends are available
  • Lastly, there's a posting area, where you can post pictures from the events and your friends can save them as well.
